Meet the Founder
Founder & Director
Mr. Prem Sai Bhagat Sir
Founder & Director, Chinhari Models

Prem Sai Bhagat is the Founder and Director of Chinhari Models and serves as Chhattisgarh's first State Pageant Director.

He began his journey at the state level within Chhattisgarh, where he won the title of Mister Fame of Chhattisgarh 2023. Notably, he is the first male model from the Raigarh district, a historic distinction.

He aspires to elevate Chhattisgarhi art and culture, as well as the state's pageant industry, to an entirely new level, thereby ensuring that Chhattisgarh's cultural heritage leaves a distinct and lasting impression on the global stage.

Furthermore, he aims to provide a superior pageant platform for future generations of models and contestants, with the ultimate goal of establishing Chhattisgarh's reputation in the pageant world at both national and international levels.
