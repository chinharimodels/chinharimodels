Our Vision

Empowering Talent

A Launchpad for Dreams

Founded by Mr. Prem Sai Bhagat, Chinhari Models is Chhattisgarh's trusted modeling and event management agency. We have become a launchpad for talent and a creative force behind fashion events, campaigns, and pageant-driven experiences across the state.

Our vision is rooted in Chhattisgarhi pride and modern professionalism, ensuring that our cultural heritage leaves a distinct impression on the global stage.
