Mister & Miss Chhattisgarh 2026

Chinhari Models invites emerging and professional talent from all across Chhattisgarh to participate in the state's premier fashion pageant. Below are the complete round structures, timeline, and rules. Fill out the application form at the bottom to register.

📅 Timeline & Fees

  • Online Registration: 1 July – 31 July 2026
  • Registration Fees: ₹999/- (Non-Refundable)
  • Final Audition & Selection: 9 August 2026
  • Show/Finalist Fee: ₹2,500/- (Payable only after selection as a finalist)
  • Grooming Sessions: 29 August 2026 (Expert motivational guidance)
  • Grand Finale: 30 August 2026

🌟 Pageant Selection Rounds & Finale

Round 1: Screening
• Registration Form Fillup & Verification
• Document verification & Q/A session

Round 2: Audition Video
• Short Video Rampwalk & Introduction
• Submission of 3 recent photographs

Round 3: Talent & Sash Ceremony
• Finalist Selection and sash ceremony
• Dress Code:
  - Boys: White Shirt & Blue Jeans
  - Girls: White Top & Blue Shorts/Jeans

Round 4: Instagram & Media
• Evaluate Instagram prominence & reach
• Highest post/reels engagement analysis
• Feature on the official finalist poster

Round 5: Popularity Fast-Track
• Popularity and deposit evaluation
• Highest earner/depositor in 24 hrs directly qualifies for the Top 7

Grand Finale Show Format:
• Round 6: Traditional Wear Round
• Round 7: Creative Wear Round
• Round 8: Western Wear Round (Walk & Intro)
• Round 9: Top 7 (Walk & Final Q/A)
• Round 10: Top 3 (Verdict Q/A & Crowning)
